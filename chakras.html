Chakras

Chakras, or disks in sanskrit are believed to be energy points throughout our body. There are over 100 but we will focus on the seven major chakras in ascending order root, sacral, solar plexus, heart, throat, third eye, and crown. The purpose of yoga when it comes to chakras is to create balance with each one. As the site grows so will the information.

Crown

Found at the peak of our head and above. It is denoted by a white light or violet. This represents our spiritual awareness.

Third Eye

Found in the space between our brows or third eye and denoted by the color indigo or deep blue. This is our intuition or ability to see and recognize our path in life.

Throat

Found in the throat and denoted by a light blue color. Represents speech and self-expression and how to communicate with others.

Heart

Found in the heart and denoted by the color green. This chakra represents our ability to love and be loved unconditionally.

Solar Plexus

Found in the naval or belly button and denoted by the color yellow. Connects us to our ego, self-worth, and how we think we are perceived by others.

Sacral

Found in the genitals and denoted by an orange wheel. Connects us to joy, life, and the remover of inhibitions.

Root

Found in the perinium and denoted by a red wheel. This is the chakra of self-awareness and physical identity.
